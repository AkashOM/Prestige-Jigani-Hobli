Overview

Prestige Jigani Hobli Overview

Experience opulent living in the heart of Anekal Taluk, Bangalore, at the Prestige Jigani Hobli . Situated across 56.29 acres of lush landscapes, this prestigious residential development is a testament to luxury living. In the ever-evolving district of Jigani Hobli, you'll find yourself amidst a bustling hub of innovation and development. Notably, the Electronic City, one of Bangalore's largest IT parks, is in close proximity, ensuring convenience for tech-savvy professionals. Venture a bit further, and you'll discover the tranquil haven of Rajapura Village, known for its picturesque beauty and serene lakes. Prestige Jigani Hobli offers a plethora of premium villas and apartments, designed with uncompromising standards to provide residents with a life of sophistication and comfort. Here, you'll experience world-class amenities, including a grand clubhouse, a refreshing swimming pool, exquisitely landscaped gardens, and various sports facilities. The location is a standout feature of this project, seamlessly connected to the rest of Bangalore through the NICE Road and Outer Ring Road, making it an ideal choice for both residential and commercial ventures. Furthermore, with the upcoming Bangalore-Mysore Expressway on the horizon, connectivity is set to reach new heights. Prestige Jigani Hobli epitomises luxury, poised to provide you with a grand lifestyle in a rapidly growing district, ensuring a prosperous future. Nestled within the charming landscapes of Jigani and Rajapura villages in Anekal Taluk, Bengaluru, Prestige Jigani Hobli boasts unparalleled connectivity via the NICE Road, Outer Ring Road, and the upcoming Bangalore-Mysore Expressway, making it a prime choice for residents and businesses alike.
